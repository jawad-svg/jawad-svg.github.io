Selected Work

Projects & case studies

Four projects that show how I work — from building governance frameworks to delivering executive-ready dashboards.

Power BI Dashboard Suite — BKK
Bakhabar Kissan (BKK) · 7 Production Dashboards
  • Target vs Achievement — Field Ops
    Monthly/yearly breakdown by district and Field Officer. Dual targets across revenue and margin. IB vs NIB sales split — fundamentally different margin profiles. Drillthrough pages per FO: top 5 products, transaction-level detail, customer/farmer line items, units, revenue and margin.
  • Products Report
    Full portfolio breakdown across Manufacturer → Category → Product hierarchy. Per-item: sales, margin, sale contribution %, margin contribution %, margin%. Smart IB/NIB toggle — combined view by default, auto-filters entire report on selection.
  • Performance Report — Field Ops Efficiency
    Unit ratio engine for FO and district efficiency. Tracks attendance, field presence, conversion. Ratios: Farmers/FO, Margin/FO, Revenue/FO, Transactions/FO, Visits/FO (business-generating vs simple). Ticket size: Revenue/Farmer, Margin/Farmer — segmented for business-generating vs overall farmers.
  • Inventory Management Dashboard
    National inventory picture across all districts and FOs. In/out tracking, stock qty and value. Highest/lowest selling, highest margin and most profitable products. Inventory card on weighted average method. Stock level monitoring.
  • Digital / E-commerce Intelligence System
    Multi-stream sales and margin tracking across company mobile app, Shopify store, and website. Conversion rates per stream. Marketing analytics by channel (Google Ads, Meta, TikTok) — spend, clicks, customer conversion. Organic vs inorganic segmentation. Customer-level metrics and full product/category/manufacturer breakdown.
  • Mari Petroleum CSR Dashboard
    Nutrition and BMI tracking for children across health programme areas. Population-level health outcome reporting built for Mari Petroleum to evidence CSR investment impact.
  • C-Level Executive Dashboards
    KPI tracking, target vs actual, sales trends. Complex DAX, full data modelling, RLS with user identification. Deployed on Windows + Linux VM gateway — rare infrastructure depth for a BI Analyst role.
